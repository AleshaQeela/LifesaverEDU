Be calm. Act fast. Save lives.
When every second matters... know what to do. This section guides you through the critical first steps during an emergency before medical help arrives.

1. Check the Scene

Make sure the area is safe for you and the injured person.

>Pastikan kawasan selamat untuk anda dan mangsa.

2. Call for Help (Dial 999)


Call emergency services immediately if the person is unresponsive, not breathing, or has severe bleeding.

Hubungi perkhidmatan kecemasan (999) dengan segera jika mangsa tidak sedarkan diri, tidak bernafas, atau mengalami pendarahan teruk.

3. Check for Life-Threatening Conditions


Use the "Tap and Shout" method to check for responsiveness. Look for breathing and major bleeding (max 10 seconds).

Gunakan kaedah “Tepuk dan Jerit” untuk semak kesedaran. Periksa pernafasan dan pendarahan besar (maksimum 10 saat).

4. Give Appropriate Care


Provide care based on your training level – such as CPR, applying pressure to wounds, or using AED if available.

Beri bantuan berdasarkan tahap latihan anda – seperti CPR, tekan luka berdarah, atau guna AED jika ada.

5. Keep Calm & Wait for Help


Reassure the victim. Stay with them and monitor their condition until EMS arrives.

Tenangkan mangsa. Temani mereka dan pantau keadaan hingga bantuan tiba.
